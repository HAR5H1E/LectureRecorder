This paper aims to examine the predictions made using the flat earth model provided in order to document the results of observations made to test whether these predictions are accurate The observations focus on whether sunlight can be seen illuminating clouds below the horizon 
as well as whether the Southern Cross constellation can be observed in the night sky. The results show that sunrise and sunset do occur, and that the Southern Cross is not visible from the observation location.
